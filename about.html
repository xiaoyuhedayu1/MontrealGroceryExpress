How It Works

Here’s how Montreal Grocery Express works:

  • Select Your Item: Navigate to the store page and write the item you want to order.
  • Add to Cart: Once you've chosen your items, add them to your cart.
  • Access the Flyer: Click on the store logo to view their flyer for more details.
  • Review Your Order: Click on your cart to double-check your items.
  • Place Your Order: Click the submit button to finalize your order.
  • Return to Homepage: Click the Montreal Grocery Express logo to go back to the homepage.

Delivery Schedule

We deliver your groceries every two weeks, ensuring you have everything you need without the hassle of frequent shopping trips. Our service is designed for convenience and reliability, bringing the grocery store directly to your doorstep on a bi-weekly basis. You can view the delivery date in your cart for easy reference.

Pricing

Our service fee is 15% of your total order, ensuring you get the best value while benefiting from our reliable delivery service. This fee covers the convenience of having groceries delivered directly to your door every two weeks.
